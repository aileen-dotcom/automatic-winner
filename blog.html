About Me

Hi, I'm Aileen! I'm a detail-oriented and tech-savvy professional with a diverse background spanning data administration, healthcare, education, and financial services. I bring experience in HRIS systems, data accuracy, and empathetic communication across culturally diverse environments.

My journey has taken me from managing international conferences and advisory at University to supporting healthcare teams during challenging times, earning recognition including the COVID-19 Response Recognition Award. With qualifications ranging from a Masters degree to certifications in Microsoft Excel, AWS Cloud Practitioner, and Machine Learning, I'm passionate about leveraging technology to create meaningful solutions.

When I'm not diving into data or coding in GitHub, you'll find me staying active through swimming, volleyball, and dancing. I love expressing my creative side through sketching, calligraphy, accordion, and photography. I actively volunteer in the local communities, believing that giving back strengthens us all.

This blog captures my ongoing job search journey, professional insights, and the lessons I'm learning along the way. Let's connect and grow together in this ever-evolving tech landscape!
